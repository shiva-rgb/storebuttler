About Martify

Martify is a comprehensive online store management platform designed to help store owners easily manage their inventory, process orders, and provide a seamless shopping experience for their customers.

With Martify, you can:

  • Upload and manage your inventory using Excel or CSV files
  • Track and process customer orders efficiently
  • Customize your store details and settings
  • Control your store's live status with a simple toggle
  • Provide a modern, user-friendly shopping experience for your customers
